The "Eco" cluster (5 listings all using "Party planner") is almost certainly a lead-gen network.

5. **Real competition is thin.** Only 4-5 businesses have real reviews and real websites: Pro Waste (92 reviews), Service Sanitation (331 reviews, but Gary IN), Elegant Restrooms (47 reviews), Twilight (14 reviews), and Munoz (5 reviews). Palace has room to dominate with a properly optimized listing.
